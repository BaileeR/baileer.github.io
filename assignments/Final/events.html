This Year's Event: Cycle the Continental Divide

The event will be held May 4th - May 15th. Bikers will start in Rooseville, Montana and will end in Polaris, Montana. The trail is 540 miles long and Bikers will travel 50 miles a day. Food and water will be provided. Riders are welcome to bring their own camping gear or use the provided gear.

Biker Requirments:

  • 18 or older
  • A bicycle in good condition
  • A backpack or other storage container
  • Ability to bike 50 or more miles a day
  • Any personal medication

Prices:

No Perks, Just the Ride - $50

Tour Book, Maps, and a Photobook - $70

Late Fee - Register 2 weeks before the event - 90$
